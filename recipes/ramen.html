Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a simple ramen recipe that you can make at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of instant ramen noodles
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ginger powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 boiled eggs, halved

Steps

  1. In a medium pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Add the instant ramen noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes until they are tender. Drain and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add the soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic powder, ginger powder, and red pepper flakes (if using). Stir to combine.
  4. Add the cooked noodles back into the pot and toss to coat them in the sauce.
  5. Divide the noodles into bowls and top with sliced green onions and halved boiled eggs.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
